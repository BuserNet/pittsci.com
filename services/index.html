Services

At PittSci we strive to provide you with prompt, professional service.  Whether it is a normal repair,  preventative maintenance, or emergency service, we are available 24/7.

Quotes can usually be provided same day as diagnosis, as long as the manufacture has stock.  Most equipment between 7-10 years are commonly in stock.

We work with all manufactures and/or brands of equipment.  Technicians have factory training on certain brands, which can be used to work on the same type of equipment by other manufacturers.

The following is a list of some of the types of equipment we work on:

incubators              ware washers                   autoclaves

centrifuges             -80 c freezers                  stability chambers

lyophilizers             cryo-tanks                        heating cabinets

thermocyclers         shakers                           water and bead baths

vacuum pumps       RO water systems        cryo tomes

PCR machines         cooler/freezers reach-in and walk-in

gel imaging            co2 systems                    clean rooms

This list is not exhaustive; PittSci works on any clinical lab or research equipment the customer requires.

We are qualified to work in BSL 1, BSL2, and BSL3. We are proficient in the procedures and protocols to work in and around these facilities.
